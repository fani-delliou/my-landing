Our Philosophy

It is like no other gym in town. You can be anyone you want to be. Be any shape you happen to be. And be a man, woman or completely undecided. We don't care. If you are serious about working out and having a laugh we are happy to accommodate. There are no disapproving glances, rolled eyeballs, tutts, sighs or potted palms round our place!

Ownership

Fani Delliou is a certified personal trainer who spent all her early career working with models and actors getting them fit for the runway and the spotlight. In 2010 she decided it was time to make herself available to all crowds and now just the elite, thus she opened Beast Mode Gym in New York. Over the period of 10 years the brand has expanded and is now available in London and Paris as well.
